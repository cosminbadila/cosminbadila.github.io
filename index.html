About Us

Here is a short story About Us

We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee. We offer a delicious variety of coffee made by our professionally trained employers. We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages. All of our sauces & syrups are made in-house with all natural ingredients (no chemicals or preservatives) ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health. You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker. We look forward to serving you at our Coffee Shop.
